GTM Tracker

Usage

Add GTMTracker in your page

<script type="text/javascript" src="gtm-tracker.js"></script>

GTMTracker init

GTMTracker.init({
	'event': 'eventTracking',
	'eventCategory': 'your event category'
});

In your tag link

<a href="#" data-gtm="eventAction, eventLabel">Test2</a>
